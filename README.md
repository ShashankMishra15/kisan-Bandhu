Kisan Bandhu
Kisan Bandhu is a Smart Crop Advisory and Local Marketplace platform designed to support farmers in making informed agricultural decisions.

Problem Statement
Many farmers face challenges in selecting suitable crops based on soil type, season, rainfall, and regional conditions. In addition, access to accurate government scheme information and direct market connections is often limited, especially for small and marginal farmers. These challenges can result in inefficient crop planning and reduced profitability.

Proposed Solution
Kisan Bandhu is a rule-based agricultural decision-support platform that provides:

Crop recommendations based on soil type, season, rainfall, and region
Basic irrigation and fertilizer guidance
Government scheme awareness and MSP information
A simple local marketplace for crop listings
The system focuses on clarity, accessibility, and practical implementation within a structured and modular design.

Key Features
Rule-based Crop Recommendation Engine
Risk Alerts and Basic Farming Guidance
Government Schemes Information Section
Minimum Support Price (MSP) Awareness
Farmer-to-Buyer Listing Module
Simple and User-Friendly Interface

Impact:
Kisan Bandhu aims to:

Improve crop decision-making for farmers
Increase awareness of government agricultural schemes
Provide easier market access to small-scale farmers
Reduce dependency on informal intermediaries
Encourage digital adoption in rural agricultural ecosystems
By combining advisory logic with basic marketplace functionality, the platform promotes informed and structured agricultural planning.
